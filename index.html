Welcome

Hi, I am Laura, and a warm welcome to my Cross Stitch Art website!

Love reignited

I first encountered cross-stitching while at school during my home economics class and have not lifted a needle until a decade later when I met my friend's mum.


I visited their home and almost lost my breath as her house walls were all covered by a vast amount of cross stitch designs she has done over the years.


That's when I thought to try cross-stitching again. And rest is history as they say...

Finding my niche

At first I started with a couple of generic cross-stitch designs, including various small scenic designs, one of which was by a French Impressionist artist, Claude Monet's "Poppies near Argenteuil, 1875".


It was at then that the penny dropped for me and I set out to start my journey into recreating the great Old Master's paintings in a cross-stitch. I could not have thought of a better starting point than recreating my favourite artist, Edgar Degas', ballet scenes.


Since then, I have completed two paintings of his and am currently finishing a third design of his.


I recorded the progress of the two latest projects in my portfolio section, alongside other designs that I have completed.
